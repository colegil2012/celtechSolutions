About

We build the technology that powers good businesses.

Celtech Solutions is a small, focused technical consultancy. We partner with founders, operators and product teams to take ideas from sketch to shipped — and then to keep them running well.

Our work spans web platforms, APIs, data integrations and developer tooling. We favour boring, durable choices, clear communication, and code we'd be happy to maintain ourselves (because often, we will).
